    Enter Official Cups completely free of charge! Take part in our growing range of groundbreaking competitions without denting your Power Token balance.

    Win Power Tokens by entering your team into our weekly Prized Cups at discounts of up to 55%!

    Free Challenges and Scout Matches

    Take on any team anytime without touching your Power Tokens. Regular Club Members receive 10 free Challenges or Scout Matches every month (Gold Club Members receive 15), so you can battle best friends, try out new tactics and challenge champions free of charge.

    Extra Tactics Slots

    Prepare for every eventuality on the pitch and increase your tactical choices for each match thanks to seven bonus Tactic Slots and the option to rename each Tactic.

    LiveScores

    Follow your favourite team’s games, goal-by-goal in real-time! Stay up-to-date with score flashes delivered directly to your Clubhouse and Office and during 3D matches.

    National Coach Eligibility

    Lead your nation to victory in international competition. Become eligible to be voted your country’s National Coach and manage your National Team in the World Cup.

    Training Reports & Graphs

    Perfect your Training strategy with loads of valuable data. Our special reports and graphs store and display all sorts of information from your players’ Training histories.

    Transfer Monitor

    Stay one step ahead of your rivals in the transfer market! Thanks to our informative Transfer Monitor, you can keep a constant eye on all your current deals.

    My Searches

    Slash the time you spend looking for new players! With the My Searches feature, you can save your own custom searches to use again and again.

    Player Shortlist

    Snap up your top transfer targets before your rivals thanks to a comprehensive monitoring tool that lets you keep tabs on an extra 25 players across the ManagerZone world.

    Youth Name Change

    Customise your roster by choosing the names of your best Youth players. Help your players stand out from the crowd by selecting personalised names from our vast player name database.

    Badge Editor

    Customise your club’s badge using our built-in editor. Choose from an extended range of backgrounds, patterns and colours to create a crest worth kissing!

    Kit Editor

    Make sure your players stand out from the crowd by choosing your home, away and keeper kits from an increased array of fashionable designs.

    Statistics

    Receive round-the-clock access to all your club’s vital statistics to keep your players performing at the top of their game. A must-have for all modern managers!

    Player Notes

    Keep a record of every squad member’s requirements, from training needs to tactical tweaks, with this handy player-specific notebook tool.

    Player Availability Tool

    Never lose on walkover again! See all player eligibility information (Age, Cards, Injuries, etc.) in one clear display and set your Default Tactics with ease knowing you have sent your best available squad to the pitch.
